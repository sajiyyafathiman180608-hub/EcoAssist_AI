Sustainability means meeting current needs without harming future generations.

Climate Action:
Climate change is caused by greenhouse gas emissions. People can help by reducing energy use, using renewable energy, and protecting forests.

Waste Management:
Reduce, reuse, and recycle waste. Avoid single-use plastics and separate recyclable materials.

Water Conservation:
Save water by fixing leaks, reducing unnecessary usage, and using water-efficient methods.

Energy Conservation:
Use LED lights, turn off unused devices, and choose clean energy sources.

Sustainable Cities:
Sustainable cities focus on efficient transport, waste management, green spaces, and responsible resource use.
